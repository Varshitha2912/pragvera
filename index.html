About Us

PragVera is a consulting firm backed by over 250 years of collective experience, delivering end-to-end solutions tailored to enhance business performance. Our expertise spans global supply chain management, where we specialize in cost-optimized sourcing strategies, program management, supplier development, and quality assurance. We offer strategic Board Advisory services and drive transformation through business process re-engineering, using a balanced scorecard approach. Our capabilities also include manufacturing and operational excellence, EXIM and international logistics, as well as comprehensive risk assessment and management across supply chains. Additionally, we support organizations with shared services solutions built on a variable cost model, ensuring agility and scalability.
